Learn more about Toki Pona with us!

Toki Pona, an isolating language with 14 phonemes, embraces minimalism by focusing on simple, universal concepts to convey meaning with a small vocabulary. In "Toki Pona: The Language of Good," Lang introduces about 120 words (pu), while the Toki Pona Dictionary lists 137 essential words and additional less-used ones (ku). The language's simplicity in pronunciation serves as a cross-cultural bridge, though it wasn't initially designed as an international auxiliary language.


Inspired by Taoist philosophy, Toki Pona aims to help users concentrate on fundamental aspects of life, promoting positive thinking in line with the Sapir–Whorf hypothesis. Despite its limited vocabulary, speakers effectively communicate, relying on context and word combinations for specific meanings.


Since its creation, a small online community of Toki Pona speakers has formed, with occasional in-person meetings during the 2000s and 2010s complementing the predominant online interactions in chat rooms and social media.
